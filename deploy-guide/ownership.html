Sign-off role
DevOps carries the heaviest primary load (24 steps) because Part 1 and most of Part 3 are infrastructure-only work with no banking-specific logic — this is identical to the pattern in the original HR deployment guide. AI/ML and Backend are evenly balanced (12 steps each) since every use case needs both an agent build and an integration build. Credit Risk Analyst and Compliance Officer carry few primary steps by design — their leverage is concentrated in review/sign-off authority at the highest-risk gates.
