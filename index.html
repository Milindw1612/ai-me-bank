Final Recommendation
Pitch Order: (1) MSME Credit CAM Prep — universal consensus across every LLM consulted, largest strategic upside, directly grows a stated bank priority segment. (2) Fraud Detection — near-universal consensus, fastest payback, protects existing revenue. (3) Collections Recovery — narrower consensus but strongest per-use-case numbers; excellent complementary "quick win" alongside the other two.
To the CEO: Every AI model consulted independently converged on MSME credit automation and fraud detection as the top two opportunities — that convergence is itself a strong signal of low execution risk and broad organizational fit. Start with MSME CAM Prep: it is the single most-recommended use case, ties directly to Ai-ME BANK's stated MSME growth strategy, and shows measurable TAT and revenue impact within one quarter. Fraud Detection should follow immediately after as a parallel low-risk track, with Collections Recovery as the fast-follow "prove it, then scale" initiative.
